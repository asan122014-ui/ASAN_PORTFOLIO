ABOUT ASAN

Connecting Parents and Drivers Through Smarter Technology.

ASAN is a modern child tracking platform designed to keep parents connected with every journey through live GPS tracking and real-time trip updates.

Drivers use the ASAN Driver App to manage daily trips while parents stay informed through the Parent App with live location, ETA updates and notifications.

Everything is securely connected through the ASAN Admin Portal, ensuring smooth communication and reliable daily operations.

OUR PLATFORM

One Platform. Three Connected Experiences.

ASAN brings together parents, drivers, and admin control through three different ASAN platforms that simplifies communication, child tracking, and daily operations.

Navigate daily routes, manage trips, share live location and keep parents updated throughout every journey.
